Reduce, Reuse and Recycle

Reduce, Reuse and Recycle, these three R's of the environment is the great way to protect our environment by reducing waste, saving money and less consume of energy.

So what exactly is reduce, reuse and recycle?

  • Reduce:It simply means to buy and consume less. Reducing is the most effective of the three R's. Two of the examples of reducing are buying a used good and consuming less electricity.
  • Reuse:Reusing is the use of items in other ways instead of throwing out the items that you don't needed. Reusing is the 2nd effective of the three R's.
  • Recycle:The last one is recycling. Recycling is to convert waste materials (such as plastic, bottled water, glass, broken materials and more) into a new products.

Benefits of the three R's of environment

  • saves money and electricity
  • less waste sent to landfill which reduce greenhouse gases
  • prevents pollution by reducing raw materials
  • allows waste materials to be used to create new products
  • conserves natural resources
  • it helps create jobs by recycling
  • prevents loss of biodiversity
