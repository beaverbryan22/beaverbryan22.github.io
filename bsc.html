Be a Smart Consumer

How to become a smart consumer

Buying and consuming high quality products but spending less money is the objective of a smart consumer. As a smart consumer, you buy things you really need and avoid unnecessary items so that you don't waste the resources from earth. Also you Avoid buying souvenirs because these souvenirs came from the animals that harmed the habitat of biodiversity.

What to buy and what not to buy

Buy things that will help biodiversity such as:

  • Local foods. Buy local foods since it gives good benefits to us and the environment.
  • Organic foods. Foods that are 100% pesticides free.
  • Recycled products. This products used unprocessed materials provide environmental benefits that protect our environment
  • Long lifetime products. Products that you can use for a long period of time.
  • Essential products. Products that you need for your everyday life such as toothbrush, clothes, foods, water and more daily products.

Do not buy these things that threatened biodiversity:

  • Wildlife products. These products was came from different species that affects biodiversity
  • Animal Souvenirs. Don't buy souvenirs unless it's not made from ivory skin, fur, bone, shell, beak or hooves of an endangered species.
  • Pesticides. Pesticides caused the population decline which threatens species. Don't buy it unless you have severe plant damage caused by pests.
